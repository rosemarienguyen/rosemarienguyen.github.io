Signoture Bakery

Hello! My name is Julie Signo. Since I was young I have loved baking sweets and giving out goodies for others to enjoy. These past few years in college have given me a chance to explore new flavors and venture into different kinds of treats; I love Asian-inspired desserts, creating Vietnamese Iced Coffee Macarons and Milk Tea Cupcakes.

Now, I want to expand beyond my friends and family and share these treats with the community. I aim to satisfy everyone's sweet tooth by customizing orders and keeping prices affordable. Feel free to browse the website and Instagram Page for baked goods I have made in the past! Don't see what you're craving? Contact me and I will make it happen uwu
